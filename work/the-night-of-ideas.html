THE NIGHT OF IDEAS

created all graphic content for the festival The Night of Ideas (produced in conjunction with La Nuit des Idées, hosted by the Institut Français)


LOGO

Logo created for the Los Angeles Night of Ideas plays to the theme of "utopias", harmonizing three distinct colors to unite, creating one cohesive image.


LOGO ITERATIONS

For this event, I provided two options for the logo: one more minimalist option and another more whimsical option.


FOLDABLE POSTER-PROGRAM

Created a poster that folds into a program for The Night of Ideas LA. Includes the logo as a cover page, a map of all galleries, the program of events, a poster for attendees to use as a souvenir, and a page highlighting all sponsors for the event. All colors used in the poster-program are colors found in the logo created for The Night of Ideas.


FOLDABE POSTER-PROGRAM | MAP

Created a map for the event that highlights which galleries at the Bergamot Station were used, which roads could be used to access the event, and where parking was available..


FOLDABLE POSTER-PROGRAM | PROGRAM

Created a program for the event highlighting in the blue logo color the time slots for each event. The program also highlights ongoing exhibitions at each gallery and is organized by time slot.


FOLDABLE POSTER-PROGRAM | SPONSORS

Created a sponsors page to be displayed on the backside of the poster-program. Sponsors organized by amount donated to the event.


FOLDABE POSTER-PROGRAM | POSTER

Poster utilizes all three colors from the original logo created as well as a skyline of Los Angeles (created as a separate, but similar visual identity). Poster folds up into the poster-program.


POSTCARD

Created a postcard for The Night of Ideas that includes the logo on the front side and information about the event on the back (as well as a few of the many sponsors).


WEBSITE

Created the visual identity of the Los Angeles skyline for the website nightofideasla.com with the name and theme of the event placed inside the skyline. Website includes a program of the event, a page for the featured speakers, and an organized content block of all sponsors for the event.


LA TIMES HALF-PAGE ADVERTISEMENT

Created an advertisement for The Los Angeles Times' website. The half-page advertisement (to be shown on the right-hand side of the website) includes basic information about The Night of Ideas, a list of key speakers/performers at the event, its location, website, and three logos at the bottom (its own logo, the French embassy's logo, and the logo of La Nuit des Idées). This graphic also includes the visual identity of the Los Angeles skyline, the name of the event, and its theme "utopias".


LA TIMES LEADERBOARD ADVERTISEMENT

This advertisement, also created for The Los Angeles Times' website, is in a smaller, leaderboard size, to be shown at the top of the computer screen alongside other advertisements. This includes the name of the event, the date and location, and the logo accompanied with graphic lines.


EMAIL BLAST

Designed an email blast that was to be sent out to everyone on the mailing list of the French Cultural Affairs Office - Los Angeles. Email blast includes the visual identity of the Los Angeles skyline, information about the event including key speakers, the date and location of the event, the logo, and all of the logos of the sponsors for the event.


COVERPHOTO

Designed a coverphoto for the event's Facebook page. Coverphoto includes the logo, graphic lines, the date, and the location.
