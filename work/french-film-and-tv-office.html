FACEBOOK COVER PHOTO

This cover photo, promoting five of some of the greatest French directors of the French New Wave, highlights the confluence between old and new that The Wave Film Festival France represents. The original film revolutionaries--Godard, Chabrol, Varda, Rohmer, and Truffaut--are juxtaposed against the names of each of the directors of The Wave Film Festival France.
