SHOOT THE BOOK!

created graphic content for the event Shoot the Book! 2017 and 2018, a pitching session for French authors to pitch their books to filmmakers.


SHOOT THE BOOK! 2017

This booklet's cover is an homage to books and the Abstract Expressionist movement, particularly Wassily Kandinsky. It plays with the general form of a book, but abstracts the shape and layers different shapes on top of each other using various opacities.


PROJECT DETAILS

This 28 page booklet, inspired by the 2017 Cannes' Shoot the Book! visual identity, includes:

a 33 x 81 inch kakemono
a 8 x 8 foot step-and-repeat
a Variety magazine half-page advertisement
2 save the dates
2 invitations to the event
and press release design/formatting.


SHOOT THE BOOK! 2018

This year's booklet follows a modern art deco theme with its bright colors and bold, geometric shapes.
