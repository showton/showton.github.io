BOULEVARD DE SERIES BOOKLET DESIGN

created and designed a program for an event promoting the exchange of knowledge between French and American filmmakers on the film and TV industry for both 2017 and 2018


BOULEVARD 20117

The cover of this 58 page program plays with the idea of boulevards and roads, crossing under and over each other.

Included in this program are:
a schedule of the week-long event
useful information
American filmmaker and studio biographies
French filmmaker biographies
and a credits page

Created using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and InDesign


LOGO

The logo for the event uses a bold red (highlighting both the French and American flags) and a modern script font paired with a bold sans serif font.


LOGO OPTIONS AND VARIATIONS

Six logo options were presented to the client, who chose a variation of logo option number three. Of the variations of logo option number three, the client chose logo variation number two for the event.
