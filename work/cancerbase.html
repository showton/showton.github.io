CANCERBASE APP DESIGN

an app for hospice nurses to view a timeline of data for their cancer patients.


PROJECT INTENT

This project, created with Code the Change, is a cancer data timeline app that compiles data inputted from two other apps within the Cancerbase family (Medmind - tracks the dosages and amounts of medication taken and Infusions - tracks chemotherapy data) and displays it chronologically. This app allows cancer patients to track and keep a record of their data.

View the prototype!

APP INTERFACE

This project uses filters so that users can filter between the different data (Medmind and Infusion) to either see everything, or just one app. Users can also tap on cards to see them enlarged. The app contains a simple settings bar that allows users to change their profile picture, display name, and the updates they would receive.
