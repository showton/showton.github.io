TAROT TRUMP TOY BOX PACKAGE DESIGN

for this project, we were given Funko Pop toy boxes of three presidential candidates (Hillary Clinton, Donald Trump, and Bernie Sanders) to redesign with the intent of creating an argument through graphic design


PROCESS

My partner and I chose to play with the idea of mysticism and fortune telling for this redesign project, emphasizing why Trump would be unfit as President, according to us, using the tarot cards of the emperor, the hierophant (high priest), the lovers, and the fool to represent his imperial, dogmatic, narcissistic, and foolish traits. My partner did the main panel (The Emperor), while I did the other three.


MOODBOARD

we chose to stick with colors that were similar to the colors of the American flag, but slightly different to emphasize how his presidency will be so unlike others before his. We also chose the color gold to tie in the theme of tarot cards and fortune with Trump's signature gold color (which ardorns his hotels, merchandise, and house)
