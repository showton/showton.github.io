CANCERBASE APP DESIGN

an app for hospice nurses to view a timeline of data for their cancer patients.


PROJECT INTENT

For the final project of a marketing class, we had to create a pitch deck presenting various marketing techniques to the founders of the company 121C Boards, all pro bono.

The pitch deck includes:

the mission and values of the company
lock (target audience with unfilled needs) and key (the image of the product that will fill those needs)
target audience
measurable goal
competition
brand image and positioning
marketing information systems
distribution
promotion techniques (YouTube, a print ad created as homework for the class, website design, Instagram) and plans for the future.

Created using Adobe InDesign
