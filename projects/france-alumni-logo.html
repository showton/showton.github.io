FRANCE ALUMNI USA AMBASSADORS

created two logos for the France Alumni USA Ambassadors.


LOGO DESIGN

This logo uses the colors of the French and American flags to visually highlight the bridge this program provides between French and American relations. LA is shown through negative space. It is designed both in a square and long rectangular format. Two versions of the logo are included. One that is for each "Ambassador" and one for the program itself.


ORIGINAL LOGO OPTIONS

It was an iterative process designing this logo, starting very abstract and even a bit quirky, to eventually narrowing down to something simple and clean. Triangles were a unifying theme throughout all variations to hint at the shape of the Louvre in Paris.
