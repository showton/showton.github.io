THE WIKIPEDIA WORMHOLE

This piece of information visualization visualizes the top 100 searched Wikipedia articles.


THE PROJECT

As someone who often goes on Wikipedia binges, I wanted to explore what other people binge on Wikipedia. This project categorizes the top 100 searched Wikipedia articles by six categories: people, entertainment, places, event, sex, and other. Each slice in each category represents each article, and they are ordered from most popular to least popular. The height of each category is relative to the average number of article views a category has.
