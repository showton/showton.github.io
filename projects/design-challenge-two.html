GEOMETRIC PORTRAIT

The prompt of this design challenge was to create an image inspired by an art movement that only used simple shapes.


PROCESS

For this challenge, I was inspired by Cubism, specifically the geometric, fractured figures of Picasso's Les Demoiselles d'Avignon, so I decided to challenge myself by using the rigid lines of simple shapes to create an organic overall shape--a portrait of my father.
