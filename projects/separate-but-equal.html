SHORT DOCUMENTARY PITCH DECK

a designed pitch deck for a short documentary focusing on the inherent inequality between school districts due to property tax laws


PROJECT INTENT

For the final project of a documentary filmmaking class, I chose to focus on education inequality, as it is a prevalent issue within our society. Due to property tax laws, schools in richer districts receive more funding and therefore have access to better resources than schools in poorer districts. The purpose of this pitch deck is to pitch an idea to be made into a short film.

The pitch deck includes:

a background of education inequality
a background on California property tax laws
inspiration and aesthetics for the documentary
the visual style of the film
the narrative structure of the film
a production plan
schools to highlight
and the end goals of this project

Created using Adobe InDesign
