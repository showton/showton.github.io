Designing logos for the French Consulate

VISUAL DESIGN

PROFESSIONAL WORK

Overview.

This project for the French General Consulate of Los Angeles was to create a logo for France/LA student ambassadors. This logo uses the colors of the French and American flags to visually highlight the bridge this program provides between French and American relations. LA is shown through negative space.

Made with Adobe Illustrator.

The ideation.

It was an iterative process designing this logo, starting conservatively, then moving very abstract and even a bit quirky, to eventually narrowing down to something simple and clean. Triangles were a unifying theme throughout all variations to hint at the shape of the Louvre in Paris. The client eventually settled on the last logo (bottom right), which had a simple, modern look with two colored triangles and the text “LA” in between.
