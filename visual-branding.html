Overview.

This was a simple 1 day challenge for myself, to create the branding of a product from start to finish. I chose kombucha because I am an avid kombucha drinker, but have never really loved the design of the different brands.

Made with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.

The branding.

The name came from a joke between a friend and myself, as she often called kombucha “kombooty,” because of its great benefits for your gut. This then led me to come up with the tagline “Good for your gut, good for your butt!” I continued playing with this "booty" theme by naming the flavor "Booty and the Beet," a ginger, beet, and oolong tea blend. I created two logo lockups, a horizontal one and a more square one.

For the logo, I wanted to play with typography and give it a young, modern feel. I chose a slim sans-serif typeface and altered it slightly, rounding edges and expanding letterforms to give it a playful vibe. I chose to use simple vector illustration showcasing the “booty” of Kombooty with a seated female figure to further play with the joke.

I wanted the overall look to have an abstract, Matisse-inspired, cut and paste feel, so I included abstract shapes and rows of dots with the simple silhouette.
