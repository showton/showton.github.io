Designing Boulevard de Séries 2017 + 2018

VISUAL DESIGN

PROFESSIONAL WORK

Overview.

Duration: June 2017 and 2018

Tools + skills: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, print design, branding, graphic illustration, layout design

About the program: Boulevard de Séries is an annual week-long program hosted by the French General Consulate of Los Angeles that brings together French and American film and television professionals. Each booklet includes:

  • a schedule of the week-long event
  • useful information about the event
  • American filmmaker and studio biographies
  • French filmmaker biographies
  • and a credits page

Boulevard de Séries 2017.

For the very first edition of Boulevard de Séries, I wanted to really play with the idea of roads and boulevards crossing over and under each other, merging and intersecting. I created a graphic “road” pattern, but kept the colors simple so it would not be too much visual noise.

Boulevard de Séries 2018.

For the second iteration of Boulevard de Séries, I again wanted to play with the ideas of roads and streets, but wanted to slightly depart from the first booklet. So I created a cross-hatching pattern of roads that looked similar, but distinct enough to highlight that it was a different year’s cover.
