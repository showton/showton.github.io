About me

Hi I’m Sylvana, but I go by Sylvie for short! I live in sunny Los Angeles and work as the lead designer at Food is Good. Before this, I worked at the American Automobile Association (AAA) as a UX/UI designer.

I graduated from the University of Southern California’s School of Cinematic Arts as a Media Arts and Practice major, a cross-disciplinary study that blends design with code, film, animation, VR/AR, sound, and critical theory.

Outside of work you can find me cooking up a storm in the kitchen (I was a part of Masterchef Season 11’s Top 60), building furniture, or taking ridiculous pictures for my pet tree’s Instagram account, @harveythetree.

Kaleidoscope: Refocusing the Digital Spectacle

May 2019

Designed, coded, constructed, and installed a 8 foot tall Amazon Alexa structure for USC’s Media Arts and Practice 2019 Senior Thesis Showcase. The Android Mystique questions our relationship to artificial intelligence and why subservient, secretarial roles are assigned to women by giving complete control of your experience to Alexa. Through a custom-coded skill, Alexa has the power to ask you questions, flash different colored lights, and choose which questions she feels like answering, and which she doesn’t.
